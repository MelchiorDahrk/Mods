This patch lowers the power of the robe enchantments to be more similar to enchantments in the vanilla game:

Novice Robes:
Fortify Magicka +15pts
Fortify Skill +5pts

Master Robes:
Fortify Magicka +30pts
Fortify Skill +10pts

Arch-Mage Robes:
Fortify Magicka +50pts
Fortify Intelligence +10pts
